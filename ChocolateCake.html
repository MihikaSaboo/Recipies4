Cake!!


First take a cake tin and oil it.
Now take any type of cream biscuits, eg: Oreo, BourBoun etc.
Crush the biscuits in a mixer.
Add milk little by little to the biscuit crumbs to make a paste
Add the paste to the oiled cake tin
Place the cake tin in a cooker with a lifted base, for 15 mins on medium flame.
Now take the cake out, allow it to cool down, decorate it and VOILA!
