Tadka Maggie!!


First take a cauldron and heat it.
Now add some oil in the couldron.
Let the oil heat for a minute and then add some spices, like; Turmeric and chilly powder.
Let the spices roast well, and add a little salt, chat masala and boiled veggies if desired
Now add water to the cauldron, according to the amount of oil and what you please.
Place the maggie that comes in the package in the cauldron.
Now allow the maggie to cook and absorb the spicies.
Finally have the maggie!!
