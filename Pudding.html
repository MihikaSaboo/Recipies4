Hide and Seek Pudding!


Make hot coffee and let it cool
Now take amul cream and remove it in a boul, add a little water and powdered suger to it.
Take hide and seek biscuits.
Once the coffee is cool, dip the biscuits in the coffee and place them in a flat,broad, glass jar.
Make a layer of the coffee soaked biscuits and then add a layer of the cream on it.
Repeat this process how many ever times desired.
Once your done with the layering, and grate some chocolate on it.
Finally, freeze the layers for a few hrs and have the pudding!!
