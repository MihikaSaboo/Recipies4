Watermelon Slushy!!


Take some seedless and fresh watermelons!!
Also take some lime juice if desired.
Crush the watermelons to make a juice and strain it.
For flavour add some black pepper and sugar.
Now add water to this if the juice looks thick, if not NO water is needed.
Now, add the lime juice if you want.
Finally, drink your slushy!! You can decorate it as well!
