Fruit Punch!!


First take cold strained black tea.
Now add Lemon,Orange,Pineapple Juice to this tea and mix.
Next, mix giner extract in this.
Take some crushed ice and place in the serving glass, which should preferably be tall.
Pour the mixture of the juices, tea and ginger extract in this glass
Finally, decorate the glass with slices of fruits. You can also add Soda for a sharp taste. Enjoy!
