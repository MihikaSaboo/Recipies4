White Sauce pasta!!


Take some pasta, and boil it and keep it aside.
Take a pan, add butter or oil, next add finely chopped garlic
Next, add some maida and mix it in the mixture of garlic and butter/oil until light brown
Now, pour milk into the pan and still until it becomes thick.
Next, add cheese to the mixture.
Now, add chilli flakes and oregano!
Finally, strain the boiled pasta and add it to the sauce.
Now, mix it ! And YAY! ITS READY!
Dont forget to decorate it with cheese/oregano/chilli flakes/ or anything else and enjoyyyy!
